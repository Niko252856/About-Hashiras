Who are the Hashiras?

The Hashiras are a group of elite demon slayers, being a hashira means you're the highest rank among the other demon slayer ranks. Initially in Season 1 Kimetsu No Yaiba we got to learn that there are 9 active Hashiras. Furthermore in The Mugen Train Movie ,when Rengoku is near death we got to learn that Rengoku's father used to be a Flame Hashira, meaning that there are retired Hashiras.

Gyomei Himejima

Gyomei Himejima is the Stone Hashira.He's often said to be the strongest among the Hashiras.Gyomei was born blind. Before becoming a hashira , he was a monk who cared for nine orphaned children in a temple. One night, a demon attacked the temple after one of the children betrayed Gyomei and let the demon in. The demon killed seven of the children, while Gyomei saved protected the last surviving child by beating the demon until sunrise with his bare fists.Despite his actions to save the child, he was wrongfully accused of the murders and was imprisoned. He was saved from execution by Kagaya Ubuyashiki who recognized the potential his strength and potential. This event left Gyomei with trauma and wary of people and fueled his resolve to protect others. He joined the Demon Slayer corps and became a Hashira after two months of training. Gyomei's Stone Breathing style has 5 forms.His weapon is a combination of a hand axe and a spiked flail,which are connected by a long chain.

Giyu Tomioka

Giyu Tomioka is the Water Hashira.He's mostly stoic and prefers to keep to himself.Giyu's backstory is tragic He and his older sister Tsutako,were the only surviving members of their family after a demon attack.On the day of Tsutako's wedding,a demon attacked, and she sacrificed herself to protect Giyu hiding him to ensure his survival.After this Giyu tried to tell the others what had happened, but they did not believe him, they sent him away thinking he was mentally ill. Giyu ran away and was eventually rescued by Sakonji Urokodaki, who became his mentor.During the Demon Slayer Final Selection, Giyu was protected by his friend Sabito, who was killed by a demon. Giyu felt immense guilt over being unable to protect either his sister or his friend and believed he was unworthy of his position as a Hashira. This led to him isolating himself from the other Hashiras.Giyu's Water Breathing Style has 11 Forms.

Kyojuro Rengoku

Kyojuro Rengoku is the Flame Hashira. He's an enthusiastic cheerful man.Rengoku's Backstory revolves about Rengoku being the eldest son of his family that had a long tradition of producing Flame Hashira.His Father Shinjuro Rengoku, was a former Flame Hashira who unexpectedly retired after his wife's passing, leading to him being deppressed and apathetic.He stopped training Kyojuro and his younger brother Senjuro.Despite his father's neglect and verbal abuse,Kyjuro was greatly influenced by his late mother.Before she died, she instilled in him the belief that those who are born strong have a duty to protect the weak.Taking this to heart Kyojuro trained himself to become a Demon Slayer by studying an instruction book on the Flame Breathing style. He quickly rose through the ranks, eventually becoming, a Hashira.His Flame Breathing has nine forms.

Mitsuri Kanroji

Mitsuri Kanroji is the Love Hashira.She's a kind and highly empathetic girl.Mitsuri was born with a unique physical makeup that gave her immense strength,a far beyond what her size would suggest.As a child she was able to lift a heavy stone with ease, even beating sumo wrestler's in arm wrestling matches.She also had an incredible apetite, eating more than the average person. Her hair transformed to a mix of pink and green due to eating a lot of Sakura mochi. At the age of 17 she had a failed marriage. Eventually she decided to join the Demon slayer Corps with a goal of finding someone stronger than her.Her Love Breathing has 6 forms.

Muichiro Tokito

Muichiro is the Mist Hashira , hes often quiet. Muichiro Tokito was orphaned at ten years old with his twin brother, Yuichiro, after their parents died from illness and a tragic accident, respectively. Later, a demon attacked their home, leading to Yuichiro's death and Muichiro's severe head injury, which erased his memories and left him with amnesia. Found by the Ubuyashiki family, he was trained as a Demon Slayer and became the Mist Hashira. Mist breathing has seven Forms.

Obanai Iguro

Obanai Iguro is the Serpent Hashira , He isnt afraid to speak his own mind. Obanai Iguro was born into a family of thieves who worshipped a serpent demon. His traumatic childhood as a captive offering led him to despise his tainted heritage, which became the driving force for him to become a Demon Slayer. Obanai was the first male born into his clan in 370 years, and his unusual heterochromiaeyes of different colorsinterested the snake demon they served His family kept Obanai locked in a wooden cage for years while pampering him with food He was essentially being fattened up to be presented as an eventual sacrifice. When he was 12, the demon had his mouth sliced from ear to ear to make him look more like her. The demon also stalked his cage at night, leaving him in constant terror.

Sanemi Shinazugawa

Sanemi Shinazugawa is the Wind Hashira , hes Hotheaded. As the eldest of seven children, Sanemi Shinazugawa's tragic backstory began after his abusive father was killed. Their mother was then turned into a demon and slaughtered five of his younger siblings. To protect his only remaining brother, Genya, Sanemi was forced to kill his demonized mother. Genya, unaware of the situation, found Sanemi standing over their dissolving mother and accused him of being a murderer. The misunderstanding created a deep rift between the brothers. Heartbroken, Sanemi abandoned Genya and began hunting demons on his own. This fueled his intense hatred of demons, eventually leading him to become the Wind Hashira of the Demon Slayer Corps.

Shinobu Kocho

Shinobu Kocho is the Insect Hashira, She has an unsettling calmness. Shinobu Kocho's backstory is a tragic tale of loss that drives her pursuit of vengeance against demons . Her initially happy life was shattered when a demon killed her parents in front of her and her older sister, Kanae. After being saved by the Rock Hashira, Gyomei Himejima, the sisters joined the Demon Slayer Corps to prevent others from suffering the same fate. While serving in the Corps, Kanae, who had become the Flower Hashira, was fatally wounded by the Upper Rank 2 demon, Doma. With her dying breath, Kanae begged Shinobu to quit the Demon Slayer Corps, but Shinobu refused and dedicated her life to avenging her sister. To hide her burning rage, she adopted her sister's calm and cheerful outward demeanor. Insect breathing has 4 forms.

Tengen Uzui

Tengen Uzui is the Sound Hashira,He is very Flamboyant. Tengen Uzui, born into a lethal shinobi clan, was forced to survive a brutal upbringing where he lost seven siblings . Appalled by his family's heartless philosophy, he rejected his ninja background and fled with his three wives. Embracing a flamboyant and flashy personality, he joined the Demon Slayer Corps and ultimately became the Sound Hashira to protect others, a complete reversal of his dark past.
